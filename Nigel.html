Nigel HaAddmore


Yehushua Sofer (Born 1958), commonly known by his stage name 'Nigel HaAddmore', is an Israeli Musician and Martial Artist.
He is known mainly for being an Israeli Hip-Hop pioneer which released what is concidered the first Israeli Hip-Hop album of all time.
He is also known for teaching his own unique Martial Art, called "Abir Keshet".
